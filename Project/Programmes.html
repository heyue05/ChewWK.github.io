Our Programmes

Ulu Tiram International College offers a wide range of diploma and degree programmes designed to develop students' practical knowledge and industry-relevant skills. From foundation diplomas to professional bachelor's degrees, our programmes are designed to help students build solid academic and career development pathways, enabling them to excel in their chosen fields and meet the demands of a dynamic global labour market.




Diploma Programmes

Diploma in Business Management

This program is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in business operations and management. Students will learn how to apply basic management skills and strategies in a corporate environment and master core concepts such as marketing, finance, human resources, and organizational behavior. After graduation, students can enter corporate administration, marketing, operations, and other positions, or continue to study for a bachelor's degree in business administration in school.

Key Courses:

  • Introduction to Management
  • Principles of Marketing
  • Business Communication
  • Organizational Behavior
  • Fundamentals of Accounting

Diploma in Accounting

This program is designed for students who are interested in pursuing a career in accounting and finance, focusing on developing financial accounting, tax reporting, cost control and financial statement analysis skills. Graduates can work as assistant accountants, cashiers, tax assistants, or continue their undergraduate studies in finance and investment.

Key Courses:

  • Financial Accounting
  • Cost Accounting
  • Taxation
  • Auditing Principles
  • Business Law

Diploma in Information Technology

This program comprehensively explains the basics of information technology, programming, database and network management, helping students master the core technologies of the modern IT industry. After graduation, students can work as junior programmers, technical support or IT assistants, or continue to pursue a bachelor's degree in computer science and delve into software development and network security.

Key Courses:

  • Programming Fundamentals
  • Web Development
  • Database Systems
  • Networking Essentials
  • System Analysis and Design

Diploma in Digital Media Design

Combining design and technology, students will be trained in digital media creation, visual communication and web design. Courses cover graphic design, photography, animation and user experience design, cultivating talents for advertising, media and creative industries. Students can continue to pursue a bachelor's degree in digital media.

Key Courses:

  • Graphic Design Principles
  • Multimedia Production
  • Photography and Videography
  • Web Design Basics
  • UX/UI Design

Diploma in Early Childhood Education

This program is aimed at those who aspire to become kindergarten teachers or child education assistants, providing knowledge and skills in child psychological development, curriculum design, educational games and classroom management. Graduates can work in nurseries, kindergartens or preschool institutions, and can choose to continue to study for a bachelor's degree in English as a Second Language (TESL) or education.

Key Courses:

  • Child Psychology
  • Early Childhood Curriculum
  • Teaching Aids and Play
  • Classroom Management
  • Child Health and Safety

Diploma in Hospitality Management

This program is designed to develop students' management and service skills for the hotel, tourism and catering industries. Courses cover front desk management, catering services, customer relations and event planning. After graduation, students can work in hotels, resorts or travel companies, or continue to pursue a bachelor's degree in business administration.

Key Courses:

  • Front Office Operations
  • Food and Beverage Management
  • Tourism Fundamentals
  • Event Planning
  • Customer Service Excellence

Diploma in Culinary Arts

This program combines cooking skills and kitchen management knowledge to help students master the actual requirements of the modern catering industry for cooking talents. Students will learn menu design, food safety and restaurant operations. It is suitable for students who aspire to become professional chefs or start their own businesses.

Key Courses:

  • Culinary Techniques
  • Food Preparation
  • Menu Planning
  • Kitchen Operations
  • Food Hygiene and Safety

Diploma in Graphic Communication

Focusing on visual communication design and brand communication, students will learn graphic advertising, packaging design, printing and typography design, and develop practical skills required in the advertising, public relations and publishing industries. Students can also continue to study digital media or related undergraduate majors.

Key Courses:

  • Typography and Layout
  • Advertising Design
  • Branding Fundamentals
  • Print Production
  • Visual Communication



Degree Programmes

Bachelor of Business Administration

The program aims to cultivate students' comprehensive business management and leadership skills, and master business operations, strategic planning and decision-making capabilities. It is suitable for students who aspire to become business managers, entrepreneurs or enter management positions in various industries.

Key Courses:

  • Strategic Management
  • Marketing
  • Human Resource
  • Business Law

Bachelor of Computer Science

This course focuses on basic computer theory and practical application development, helping students master software design, system development and emerging technologies. After graduation, they can engage in related work such as program development, system analysis or information technology research and development.

Key Courses:

  • Software Engineering
  • Database Systems
  • Cybersecurity
  • AI Fundamentals

Bachelor of Education (TESL)

This major is aimed at talents who are interested in English teaching, focusing on English teaching methods, educational psychology and curriculum design. After graduation, you can teach in schools, language centers, education and training institutions or engage in educational management.

Key Courses:

  • Linguistics
  • TESL Methods
  • Classroom Management
  • Educational Psychology

Bachelor of Finance and Investment

This program focuses on financial management, investment analysis and risk control, cultivates students' professional qualities in the banking, securities and investment industries, and lays the foundation for students to work as financial consultants, investment analysts or corporate finance after graduation.

Key Courses:

  • Corporate Finance
  • Investment Analysis
  • Risk Management
  • Financial Markets

Bachelor of Digital Media

Combining creativity and digital technology, the programme cultivates students' multimedia design, content creation and digital marketing capabilities. After graduation, students can develop careers in media companies, creative industries or self-media fields.

Key Courses:

  • Interactive Media
  • Motion Graphics
  • Web Design
  • Creative Content Production
