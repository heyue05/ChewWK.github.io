My Favorite Game Character


Shinyou Inugami is the Special (SP) version of the Super Rare (SR) character Inugami in the game Onmyoji.

  1. The Story of Meeting SR Inugami

    2. Inugami appears in the game as a guardian yokai. He was once a stray dog ​​adopted by humans, and turned into a yokai after his beloved owner died. Despite this, he remains loyal and kind to humans.

    In the plot, Inugami is initially hostile to the player, mistakenly believing that the player is responsible for the death of his best friend. However, with the player's help, Inugami discovers the real culprit and resolves the misunderstanding. The player even turns Inugami's deceased friend into a guardian spirit to continue accompanying him. Deeply moved, Inugami calls the player “Shinyou” (meaning “True Friend”) and vows to follow and protect them as a fighting partner.




  2. The Story of Meeting SP Shinyou Inugami

    3. Shinyou Inugami is a new form that represents Inugami’s emotional growth. He was officially introduced during the Onmyoji 8th Anniversary Celebration.

    In the event plot, the Onmyoji Seimei (the player) left the courtyard for a long time (symbolizing a player who left the game). During this time, Shinyou Inugami wanders the world and discovers a realm called the “Real World.” Among many “players,” he finds Seimei again.

    Shinyou Inugami, with Seimei who has lost all memories of being an Onmyoji, embarks on a journey to reunite with their old friends. In the end, Seimei regains his memories, and they all return together to the familiar courtyard.




  3. Personality

    4. Shinyou Inugami is a loyal, gentle, and justice-driven character. He is cheerful, empathetic, and values friendship deeply. He has a strong sense of responsibility and occasionally shows a playful and cute side.

    He is like a trustworthy "big brother" who can bring comfort and security to people, and embodies the spirit of companionship and protection.




  4. Role and Features

    5. Shinyou Inugami is an SP-rarity shikigami. He retains the original Inugami's defensive mechanics and adds enhanced skills such as multi-attack, shield support, and counterattack.

    His skills combine beautiful visuals with practical combat functions, making him both a powerful attacker and a reliable supporter.




  5. In-Game Performance

    6. Shinyou Inugami performs well in combat, balancing offense and support. His versatility allows him to shine in various team compositions, whether protecting fragile damage dealers or supporting control-based teams, he always plays a vital role.
